Reanimated 4, NativeWind v4). APIs, conventions, and file structure may differ
from your training data — read the exact versioned docs at
https://docs.expo.dev/versions/v56.0.0/ before writing any code. Heed deprecation
notices (e.g. `Tabs` now lives in `expo-router/js-tabs`).

Architecture and conventions for this codebase live in `ARQUITETURA.md` — follow
them. Do not introduce a new structure or "improve" the organization on your own.
